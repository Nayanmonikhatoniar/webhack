Frequently Asked Questions

Have questions? We have answers. If you can't find what you're looking for, feel free to contact us.

The Index is a composite score derived from multiple data sources. It combines real-time sensor data (where available), historical incident records, chemical properties based on engineering models (toxicity, flammability), and machine learning predictions based on plant age, maintenance records, and geographical factors.

Our platform integrates data from public sources like the EPA (Environmental Protection Agency), academic research, open-source datasets, and partner with industrial clients who provide anonymized sensor data for more accurate, real-time monitoring under strict data privacy agreements.

Currently, our most detailed models are focused on industrial zones in North America and Europe. However, the basic risk check feature uses global databases and is available worldwide. We are actively working on expanding our high-fidelity monitoring to more regions.
